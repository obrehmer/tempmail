Why use a temporary email?

The internet used to be a quiet place. Now it’s filled with mailing lists, account verifications, and spam you didn’t ask for. But you can still take back control.

1. Protect your real inbox

Signing up for something small – like a Wi-Fi login or an online tool – shouldn’t mean giving away your personal email forever. A temporary email lets you sign up once, receive the email you need, and walk away clean.

2. No signup. No tracking.

We don’t ask who you are. We don’t store your messages after they expire. Your privacy matters. What happens in your temp inbox stays in your temp inbox — for 5 minutes.

3. Reduce spam, stay anonymous

Disposable emails are great for testing, trials, one-time downloads, or just being cautious. Why give your real identity to every newsletter or download form? Use a burner address instead.
