Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Your questions about tempmail.olifani.eu answered.

1. What is a temporary email address?

A temporary email address is a disposable, one-time-use inbox that allows you to receive emails anonymously without revealing your personal email address.

2. How long does my temporary email last?

Your temporary email address is active for 5 minutes. After that, it will be automatically deleted.

3. Can I reply to emails I receive?

Yes, you can reply directly from the email view. However, keep in mind that the address only remains active for 5 minutes.

4. How does mail forwarding work?

You can choose to forward incoming emails to your own email address. This is useful if you are on the go and cannot keep the tab open.

5. Are my emails and data secure?

Yes. All emails are stored temporarily and automatically deleted after 5 minutes. No personal data is stored permanently.

6. Can I delete the temporary address before it expires?

Yes, you can delete your temporary email address manually using the "Delete Address" feature.

7. Why should I use tempmail.olifani.eu?

To protect your real email from spam and unwanted messages. Temporary emails are perfect for one-time registrations and testing purposes.
